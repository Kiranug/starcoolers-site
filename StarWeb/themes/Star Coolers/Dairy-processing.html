Dairy Processing

  1. Industries
  2. Dairy Processing

Milk is essential for our appetite. It is widely consumed by people all over the world and is easily available through dairy farms. These dairy plants include a lot of fluid milk production for manufacturing a variety of milk products like cheese, ice cream, skimmed milk, toned milk, yogurt, butter, cottage cheese, khoa, double toned milk and many more. Milk is surely one of the nutritious supplement with a range of bacteria ;but if it is not stored properly these bacteria can spoil it.Therefore it is important that dairy industries preserve its quality to avoid bad color, odor, acidity, taste etc.

Indian Dairy Industry is contributing vastly in the economic growth of India. It is also estimated that India would have double milk processing in the coming years. Apart from this , various advanced milk processing machines are used in manufacturing plants to deliver quality products at a low cost. Dairy Industries includes various applications like fermentation, pasteurization control the climate or homogenization, these process require suitable temperature which can be provided through different coolers and condensers. So be it chilled water coil or condensers we can provide you with a variety of products to establish and maintain your dairy farm business.
