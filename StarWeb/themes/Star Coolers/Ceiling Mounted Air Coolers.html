SPECIFICATIONS

  • CIC & CIW Ranges 4000 - 50000 Kcal / hr
  • Refrigerants: Ammonia, Freon, Glycol
  • Copper tube / aluminium fin for R22 & modern equivalents.
  • Stainless steel for ammonia.
  • Fin spacings 6, 4, 3, 2 fpi.
  • Defrost: Water, Hot gas and Electric
  • Fans: AC, Aerofoil, High power EC,
  • Choice of casing, accessories & speed according to application

We design Ceiling Mounted Coolers which are customizable as per your industry needs. These ceiling mounted coolers are primarily used for cooling and chilling. It provides optimized airflow and carefully balanced coil surface and air volume which can be altered according to the application.

They are also designed with low noise options to meet your specific working conditions. We make available new high tech designs for your diverse food processing, blast chilling and many more.Being a ceiling mounted cooler, it would also give you more space to stack up goods and services in your distribution centers.

We have an excellent technical and product designer team who would work to provide you with the best possible solutions with high performance.
